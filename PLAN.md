To hit 10K/month organic:

- Average 200 visits/month per article

## Phase 2: Content Machine (Weeks 2-12)

### Content Strategy: Topical Authority Clusters

Instead of random articles, we build **topic clusters** that signal to Google we're THE authority on knives and steel.

#### Cluster 1: Knife Steel Guides (HIGHEST PRIORITY)
This is where "Steel Snob" has a natural brand advantage. Steel comparisons are searched heavily by knife enthusiasts.

**Pillar:** "Complete Guide to Knife Steel Types" (5000+ words)

**Supporting articles (Steel vs Steel comparisons):**
- S30V vs S35VN - Which Is Better?
- MagnaCut vs M390 - The Ultimate Comparison
- VG-10 vs S30V - Budget vs Premium
- D2 Steel vs S30V
- 154CM vs S30V
- 440C vs VG-10
- CPM 3V vs CPM 4V
- AUS-8 vs 8Cr13MoV - Budget Steel Showdown
- Damascus Steel - Is It Worth The Hype?
- What Is Powder Metallurgy Steel?

**Why this works:** Each "X vs Y" article targets a specific long-tail keyword with low competition but consistent monthly searches (100-1,000/mo each). Knife enthusiasts Google these constantly. Collectively they can drive 3,000-5,000 visits/month.

#### Cluster 2: Kitchen Knives (HIGHEST VOLUME)
Kitchen knife keywords have the largest search volume in the knife niche.

**Pillar:** "Complete Kitchen Knife Guide - Everything You Need" (5000+ words)

**Supporting articles:**
- Best Chef's Knife Under $50 / $100 / $200 (3 articles)
- Best Japanese Kitchen Knives (2026)
- Santoku vs Chef's Knife - Which Should You Buy?
- German vs Japanese Kitchen Knives
- Best Knife Sets for Beginners
- How to Choose a Kitchen Knife
- Best Bread Knives (2026)
- Best Paring Knives (2026)
- Best Cleaver for Home Kitchen

#### Cluster 3: Knife Care & Maintenance (HIGH SEARCH VOLUME)
- How to Sharpen a Knife with a Whetstone (Step-by-Step)
- Best Knife Sharpeners (2026) - Electric vs Manual
- How to Hone a Knife (Most People Do It Wrong)
- How to Clean and Maintain Carbon Steel Knives
- Best Knife Storage Solutions (Blocks, Magnetic Strips, Drawer Inserts)
- How to Remove Rust from a Knife Blade

#### Cluster 4: Knife Laws by State (PROGRAMMATIC CONTENT)
The site already has a "Knife Laws" category. This is a goldmine for programmatic content.

**Generate 50 state-specific articles:**
- Knife Laws in Texas - What You Can Carry

- Knife Laws in Florida...
- (etc.)

**Why:** Each state article targets "[state] knife laws" - a keyword with 500-5,000 monthly searches per state. Low competition, high intent, and EACH article can link to product recommendations. This cluster alone could drive 5,000+ visits/month.
