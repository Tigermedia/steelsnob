## Content Production Notes

### Article Template (Buyer Guide)
1. Introduction + quick pick summary box
2. Comparison table (top 5 picks)
3. Detailed review of each pick (pros/cons)
4. Buying guide section (what to look for)
5. FAQ section (with schema)
6. Conclusion + final recommendation

### Article Template (Steel Comparison)
1. Quick answer (for featured snippet)
2. Steel specs comparison table
3. Edge retention comparison
4. Toughness comparison
5. Corrosion resistance
6. Ease of sharpening
7. Price/value analysis
8. Verdict: which steel for which use case
9. FAQ section

### Article Template (Knife Laws)
1. Quick summary box (legal/illegal by type)
2. Overview of [state] knife laws
3. Legal knife types
4. Restricted/banned knife types
5. Carry laws (open carry, concealed)
6. Blade length restrictions
7. Preemption laws
8. Penalties for violations
9. FAQ
10. Disclaimer (not legal advice)
